FAQ

The zoo is open all seven days of the weak at 11:00 Am to 11:00 Pm.

The last timing of entry is one hour before Closing.

Parking is available on Mall Road gate, however a small parking is also available at Lawrence Gate. We are unable to cater large number of vehicles, so the parking gets closed when it is full. We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause and thank you in advance for your patience.

All of our animals are fed diets which are specifically designed for their needs. We therefore do not allow members of the public to feed our animals.

Yes, wheelchairs are available at Lahore Zoo on a first come, first served, basis. You have to submit your original CNIC and can have the wheel chair, CNIC will be returned at the end of the visit when you handover the wheelchair at gate. Both gates have the facility.
